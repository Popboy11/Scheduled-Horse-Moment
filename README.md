Opening this program will schedule a horse moment, and it will pop up occasionally (somewhere between every 5 minutes and 2 hours). You must contemplate for 10 seconds before closing the popup. To stop it, close the background process in task manager.

Easter eggs:

1/10 chance of 'howard existed' text to appear

1/100 chance of finding the golden horse

1/10,000 chance of finding the unobtanium horse
